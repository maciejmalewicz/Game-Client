SETTINGS

HERE YOU CAN CONFIGURE THE KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS THAT YOU ARE USING IN GAME. YOU MAY SAVE YOUR FAVOURITE SHORTCUT SET SO IT WILL BE AVAILABLE EACH TIME YOU ARE LOGGED IN, YOU MAY EXPERIMENT ON CUSTOM SETTINGS OR COME BACK TO DEFAULT ANY TIME YOU WANT. GOOD SHORTCUTS WILL GIVE SAVE YOU SOME TIME DURING THE GAME, WHICH IS THE KEY TO VICTORY! TO CUSTOMIZE YOUR SHORTCUTS, GO TO THE "CUSTOM SETTINGS" AND CLICK ON THE BUTTON REPRESENTING WHAT YOU WANT TO BIND, AND THEN PRESS A KEY ON YOUR KEYBOARD.
